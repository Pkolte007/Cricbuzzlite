Cricket Information

Cricket is a popular sport played between two teams, each consisting of eleven players. The game is played on a circular field with a rectangular 22-yard-long pitch in the center.

The main formats of cricket are Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20) matches. Test matches are the longest format, while T20 matches are the shortest.

Cricket involves various aspects such as batting, bowling, and fielding. The team that scores the most runs wins in a batting inning, while the team that takes ten wickets first wins in a bowling inning.
